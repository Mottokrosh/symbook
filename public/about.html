Symbaroum Cyclopedia

Symbook is a quick reference cyclopedia for traits, abilities, and more for Symbaroum, the awesome tabletop roleplaying game by Swedish game designers Free League.

Symbook everywhere

Available as a Responsive web app, usable on all devices.
